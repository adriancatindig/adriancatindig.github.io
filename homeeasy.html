Description

HomeEasy is an Internet of Things (IoT) home automation and security android mobile application that controls and monitors any device such as LEDs, buzzer, ultrasonic sensor, photoresistors, and temperature and humidity sensors that are connected to the internet in real-time. Additionally, it is here that users can enable outside sensor capability in accordance with their requirements developed by other individuals, users can gain valuable insights and inspiration.
